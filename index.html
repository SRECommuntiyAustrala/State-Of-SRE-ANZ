How it works
// 01

You contribute

Answer ~15 questions about your role, salary, stack, and sentiment about SRE in Australia. Takes about 5 minutes. Your company name is never shown in the report.

// 02

Community builds

Every submission makes the dataset more accurate. The more voices, the more useful the breakdown by seniority, location, stack, and industry becomes.

// 03

You get the report

Contributors get the full State of SRE ANZ report sent directly to them — salary benchmarks, stack premiums, sentiment data, and where the market is heading.

What we collect

More than just salary.
The full picture.

Most salary surveys stop at a number. This one captures the qualitative reality of SRE in ANZ — how valued engineers feel, what's challenging organisations when recruiting, how the community wants to grow.

Company names are only visible to survey administrators. The published report contains only aggregated, anonymised data.

The goal is simple: give ANZ SREs the same quality of market intelligence that engineers in San Francisco take for granted.
